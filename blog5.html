Is Your Back Aching? Simple Stretches to Relieve Lower Back Pain

Lower back pain affects millions worldwide, often caused by long hours of sitting, poor posture, or sudden strain. At Mehar Physiotherapy Clinic, we believe in a proactive approach that combines gentle movement, strengthening, and posture correction to keep your back strong.

A few effective habits include:

- Incorporating light daily stretches to release tension in the lower back.

- Maintaining good sitting posture and using supportive seating.

- Strengthening core muscles to protect the spine from future strain.

Consistent, gentle movement can help reduce pain and prevent recurrence. For personalized guidance and hands-on care, our physiotherapists can create a tailored plan that suits your needs.
