Say Goodbye to 'Tech Neck': 5 Tips for a Healthier Neck

In today’s digital world, hours spent on smartphones, tablets, and laptops can lead to “tech neck” — neck pain, stiffness, and headaches caused by prolonged forward-head posture. At Mehar Physiotherapy Clinic, we help patients prevent and manage this modern-day issue with practical, easy-to-follow strategies.

Here are five key tips:

1. Be Mindful of Your Posture – Keep devices at eye level and adjust your screen height to reduce strain.

2. Take Frequent Breaks – Short movement breaks every 20–30 minutes can keep neck muscles relaxed.

3. Gentle Neck Stretches – Light daily stretches can improve flexibility and ease discomfort.

4. Strengthen Your Upper Back – Building shoulder and upper back strength supports better posture.

5. Create an Ergonomic Workspace – Use supportive seating and arrange your desk to promote healthy alignment.

If neck pain persists, don’t ignore it. Our team can provide a thorough assessment and a personalized treatment plan to keep your neck healthy and pain-free.
